COMMON SPELLS (1st level)

CANTRIPS:
Fire Bolt: V, 30 ft., Instant. Ranged spell attack +8 to hit, 2d6 fire damage.
Eldritch Blast: S, 60 ft., Instant. Ranged spell attack +8 to hit, 2d10 force damage.
Mage Hand: S, 5 ft. radius. Touch. Creates a 5ft cube of mage hand that can move independently (3/round).
Prestidigitation: V, 10 ft. range. Minor illusion. Creates minor magical effect.

2ND LEVEL:
Sacred Flame: V, 60 ft., 1 action. Fires a cone of radiant energy, 4d6 fire damage.
Cure Wounds: S, 30 ft., 1 action. Heals 2d8 + your level HP per round for 5 rounds.
Shield: S, 30 ft., 1 bonus action. Creates a magical shield that absorbs up to 24 hit points.
Magic Missile: V, 60 ft., 1 action. Ranged spell attack +9 to hit, deals 2d8 piercing damage.

3RD LEVEL:
Detect Magic: S, 120 ft., 1 bonus action. Detects magical items within range (up to 5th level).
Hold Person: V, 60 ft., 1 action. Restrains target until dispelled or ends of turn.
Scorching Ray: V, 30 ft., 1 action. Fires a cone of fire, 4d6 fire damage.
Invisibility: S, 60 ft., 1 action. Self only. Cannot be seen.
Misty Step: S, 60 ft., 1 bonus action. Teleports self up to 60 ft.

4TH LEVEL:
Fireball: V, 120 ft., 1 action. Fires a 15ft sphere of fire, 3d8 fire damage each round.
Lightning Bolt: S, 60 ft., 1 action. Ranged spell attack +9 to hit, deals 3d10 lightning damage.
Counterspell: V or S, 60 ft., 1 bonus action. Targets a spell being cast; must be within range and have line of sight.
Dispel Magic: S, 60 ft., 1 action. Removes magical effects on target.

5TH LEVEL:
Fly: S, 20/360°, 1 action. Self only. Immune to nonmagical harm while flying.
Haste: V or S, 60 ft., 1 bonus action. Speed doubles for 1 minute.
Hypnotic Pattern: V, 90 ft., 1 action. Targets must make Wis save DC 8+prof; fails roll and is hypnotized for 1 min.
Polymorph: V, 60 ft., 1 action. Target becomes a creature of your choice (must be humanoid or beast).
Banishment: S, 60 ft., 1 action. Dispels effect on target.
Greater Invisibility: S, 360°, 1 action. Self only; cannot be seen in any light.
Wall of Fire: V, 90 ft., 1 action. Fires wall of fire up to 20ft high, deals 4d8 fire damage each round.
Cone of Cold: S, 60 ft., 1 action. Fires cone of cold, 4d6 cold damage each round.
Mass Cure Wounds: V, 30 ft., 1 action. Heals target and allies within range for 5 rounds (2d8+level HP).
Raise Dead: S, 30 ft., 1 action. Revives a dead creature.
