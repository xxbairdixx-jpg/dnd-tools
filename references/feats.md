# D&D 5e Feats

## Ability Score Improvement (ASI)
**Prerequisites:** Any ability score 8 or higher. Must be a character level up.
**Description:** You can increase one ability score by 1, up to a maximum of 20. This is the most common way to gain abilities.

## Alert
**Prerequisites:** None
**Description:** Your reaction is automatically triggered when you take damage from an enemy that is within 5 feet and you have advantage on your saving throw against fear or charm effects.

## Athlete
**Prerequisites:** Any ability score 8 or higher. Must be a character level up.
**Description:** You gain proficiency with the Athletics skill.

## Actor
**Prerequisites:** None
**Description:** Your performance is so captivating that you can draw attention from within 30 feet for 1 minute, as if you were performing in a tavern or similar setting. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn.

## Charger
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher.
**Description:** You gain the ability to use your movement to make a melee attack with disadvantage on opportunity attacks.

## Crossbow Expert
**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 13 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your advantage on Dexterity saving throws is doubled when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage.

## Defensive Duelist
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher.
**Description:** You have a +5 bonus to your AC, and you cannot take the Disengage action as part of your turn.

## Dual Wielder
**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 13 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your advantage on Dexterity saving throws is doubled when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage. You can also use two different weapons as a single attack.

## Dungeon Delver
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** You gain advantage on Intelligence saving throws related to traps and puzzles.

## Durable
**Prerequisites:** Constitution 14 or higher.
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 2, and you have resistance against nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from creatures of your choice.

## Elemental Adept
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher.
**Description:** You can cast one spell from a class that uses the same element as your racial trait once per long rest. The spell must be at least level 1.

## Grappler
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your advantage on Strength saving throws is doubled when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage. You can also use your melee weapon attack to grapple a creature.

## Great Weapon Master
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your weapon attacks have advantage on attack rolls, and you gain the ability to make two melee attacks as part of your turn.

## Healer
**Prerequisites:** Wisdom 12 or higher.
**Description:** You can use a bonus action to cast the Cure Wounds spell (once per long rest), which heals 1d8 + Wisdom modifier hit points. This is a self-care feature, not for healing others.

## Heavy Armor Master
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher.
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 2 when you wear heavy armor.

## Inspiring Leader
**Prerequisites:** Charisma 13 or higher.
**Description:** You can grant advantage on saving throws to allies within 60 feet who are incapacitated. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn.

## Keen Mind
**Prerequisites:** Wisdom 14 or higher.
**Description:** You gain proficiency in the Arcana skill, and you have resistance against psychic damage.

## Lightly Armored
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 2 when you wear light armor.

## Lucky
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher.
**Description:** Once per turn, at the end of your turn, you can reroll a d20 to take the higher result. This effect is not available on initiative rolls or attacks that use advantage or disadvantage.

## Mage Slayer
**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your weapon attacks have advantage on attack rolls against a target with your weapon type.

## Magic Initiate
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher.
**Description:** You can cast two spells from different classes at will. The choice is yours, but both must be level 1.

## Martial Adept
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your attack rolls with melee weapons have advantage when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage. You can also use your weapon to grapple a creature.

## Medium Armor Master
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher.
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 2 when you wear medium armor.

## Mobile
**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 13 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** You can take the Disengage action as part of your turn. This increases your mobility and reduces vulnerability to opportunity attacks.

## Moderately Armored
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 1 when you wear medium armor.

## Mounted Combatant
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** You can use your mount as part of your movement. Your mount must be able to move at least 30 feet per round.

## Observant
**Prerequisites:** Wisdom 12 or higher.
**Description:** You have advantage on Wisdom saving throws against being frightened, and you have resistance against psychic damage.

## Polearm Master
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your weapon attacks have advantage on attack rolls when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage. You can also use your polearm to grapple a creature.

## Resilient
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 2, and you have resistance against nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from creatures of your choice.

## Ritual Caster
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher.
**Description:** You can cast the Identify spell at will. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn.

## Savage Attacker
**Prerequisites:** Strength 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your weapon attacks have advantage on attack rolls when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage. You can also use your weapon to grapple a creature.

## Sentinel
**Prerequisites:** Wisdom 12 or higher.
**Description:** You gain the ability to make an action to cast the Shield spell at will. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn.

## Sharpshooter
**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 14 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your weapon attacks have disadvantage on attack rolls against a target that is not you.

## Shield Master
**Prerequisites:** Strength 12 or higher.
**Description:** You can cast the Shield spell at will. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn.

## Skilled
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 1 when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage.

## Skulker
**Prerequisites:** Dexterity 13 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws made to avoid falling prone, and your AC is increased by 1 when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage.

## Spell Sniper
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher.
**Description:** Your spell attacks have advantage on attack rolls against a target with your weapon type. You can only use this feature for spells that require a component of your weapon type.

## Tavern Brawler
**Prerequisites:** Strength 12 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** Your melee weapon attacks have advantage on attack rolls when you are wearing armor that grants resistance against piercing damage. You can also use your weapon to grapple a creature.

## Tough
**Prerequisites:** Constitution 14 or higher.
**Description:** Your AC is increased by 2, and you have resistance against nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from creatures of your choice.

## War Caster
**Prerequisites:** Intelligence 13 or higher, and proficiency in the Sledging skill (or equivalent).
**Description:** You can cast the Detect Magic spell at will. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn.
