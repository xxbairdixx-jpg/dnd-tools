QUICK REFERENCE: D&D 5E MECHANICS

ABILITY SCORES & MODIFIERS:
- All ability scores have a modifier = (score - 10) / 2, rounded down.
- Proficiency bonus increases by one each level after 4th.

SKILL LIST USES ABILITIES:
STR: Athletics, Intimidation
DEX: Acrobatics, Perception, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
CON: Athletics, Perception, Survival
INT: Arcana, History, Investigation, Insight, Medicine, Perception
WIS: Deception, Insight, Nature, Perception, Religion, Survival
CHA: Deception, Intimidation, Persuasion

ADVANTAGE/ DISADVANTAGE:
- Roll two dice, use the higher for advantage.
- Roll two dice, use the lower for disadvantage.

ATTACK ROLLS & AC:
- Attack roll = d20 + modifier. On 10+ hits target.
- Damage is rolled separately from attack success.
- Armor Class (AC) = base defense + armor modifiers. Must be >= 10 to not take damage.

DAMAGE & RESISTANCES:
- Damage types: piercing, slashing, bludgeoning, thumping, crushing, blinding, necrotic, poison, psychic, radiant.
- Resistances: halved damage from specific damage types (e.g., fire resistance).
- Immunities: no damage from specific type.

COVER:
- Half cover: AC +1, disadvantage on attacks against you unless attacker has advantage.
- Three-quarters cover: AC +2, same rules as half cover.
- Full cover: AC +3, same rules as three-quarters cover.

SAVING THROWS:
- Must be against a DC (ability score + proficiency bonus).
- Success = avoid effect. Failure = take effect.

CONDITIONS SUMMARY:
- Blinded/Deafened/Distracted: Con save each turn to end.
- Charmed/Frightened: Cha save at start of turn to end.
- Exhaustion: Progression as above, death at level 6.
- Grappled/Restrained: Restraint mechanics apply.

REST:
- Short rest (4+ hours): regain spell slots up to your class limit.
- Long rest (8+ hours): full recovery. Reset all conditions except exhaustion.

DEATH SAVING THROWS:
- Creatures with 0 HP make a d20 roll against DC 15 + level of highest creature in party.
- If they fail, they die. Success = survive but take damage equal to their level.

SPELL SLOTS BY LEVEL:
- Cantrips: unlimited
- 1st-level: X slots (X = class level)
- 2nd-level: X slots
- 3rd-level: X slots
- 4th-level: X slots
- 5th-level: X slots

BONUS ACTION VS REACTION:
- Bonus action: free, can be used once per turn.
- Reaction: triggered by specific events (attack, spell cast). Cannot be used to react to your own actions.
