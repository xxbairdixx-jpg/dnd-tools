# Random Tables for DMs

## Plot Hooks (d20)
1. A mysterious letter arrives at the party's inn, inviting them to a secret meeting.
2. The party encounters a lost traveler who asks for their help in finding a missing relative.
3. A local noble seeks assistance from the party in resolving a dispute with an enemy lord.
4. A wizard offers the group a chance to gain powerful knowledge by completing a dangerous quest.
5. An ancient artifact is discovered, and its true purpose must be uncovered.
6. A rumor of treasure leads the party to a forgotten tomb.
7. A bandit leader challenges the party to a duel for control of a nearby town.
8. The party uncovers evidence that their mentor has been involved in illegal activities.
9. A powerful spellcaster seeks the party's aid in casting an ancient, forbidden spell.
10. A prophecy foretells the party's involvement in a great cosmic event.

## Complications (d20)
1. The party is ambushed by a rival group of adventurers who claim to have prior claims on the treasure.
2. A local authority figure becomes suspicious of the party and demands proof of their worthiness.
3. The party's actions inadvertently trigger a natural disaster, such as an earthquake or flood.
4. A powerful creature emerges from its lair and begins pursuing the party relentlessly.
5. The party discovers that one of their own has been secretly working for an enemy faction.
6. A treacherous ally within the party betrays them at a critical moment.
7. The party's equipment is stolen, forcing them to scavenge or purchase replacements.
8. A local cult starts spreading rumors about the party's activities, causing public unrest and distrust.
9. The party must navigate through a political minefield, dealing with corrupt officials and double-crossing allies.
10. A powerful spellcaster casts an illusion that confuses the party, making it difficult to make decisions.

## NPC Motivations (d20)
1. Greed: The NPC seeks wealth and power above all else.
2. Ambition: The NPC desires recognition and status within their community or organization.
3. Revenge: The NPC is driven by a desire for vengeance against those who wronged them in the past.
4. Love: The NPC is deeply devoted to someone, often putting that person's needs above their own.
5. Duty: The NPC believes strongly in fulfilling their responsibilities and obligations to others or society.
6. Power: The NPC seeks control over resources, people, or territories to exert influence.
7. Survival: The NPC prioritizes their own safety and the well-being of those they care about above all else.
8. Loyalty: The NPC is fiercely loyal to a cause, group, or individual, even if it means putting themselves in danger.
9. Idealism: The NPC is driven by a set of beliefs or principles that guide their actions and decisions.
10. Curiosity: The NPC is driven by an insatiable desire to explore the unknown and uncover hidden truths.

## Wilderness Events (d20)
1. A sudden storm traps the party in the wilderness, forcing them to seek shelter quickly.
2. The party encounters a dangerous animal, such as a grizzly bear or a pack of wolves.
3. A group of hostile nomads ambushes the party, seeking to rob them of their supplies and equipment.
4. The party comes across an ancient ruin that is slowly collapsing around them.
5. A hidden waterfall provides fresh water for the party but requires them to navigate treacherous terrain to reach it.
6. The party discovers a natural spring that can heal minor injuries or provide temporary respite from fatigue.
7. A group of travelers offers the party shelter and information about the local area in exchange for their help with a quest.
8. The party encounters a rare plant or flower that has healing properties, but it is extremely difficult to find.
9. A mysterious fog rolls in, obscuring visibility and making navigation challenging.
10. The party stumbles upon an old campsite where they can gather supplies and rest for the night.

## Town Events (d20)
1. A local festival or celebration is taking place, drawing crowds from far and wide.
2. A notorious criminal is being tried in court, and the party must decide whether to testify against them or remain silent.
3. The town's mayor has issued a proclamation that affects the party's interests, such as new taxes or restrictions on travel.
4. A mysterious stranger arrives in town, bringing news of an urgent situation that requires immediate attention.
5. The town's blacksmith is holding a competition to see who can craft the best weapon or armor.
6. A group of travelers from a distant land seeks the party's help in resolving a dispute with local authorities.
7. The town's mayor has issued a reward for information leading to the capture of a dangerous criminal.
8. A festival or parade is taking place, and the party can participate or watch as part of their adventure.
9. An old acquaintance from their past appears unexpectedly, offering them valuable advice or assistance.
10. The town's mayor has issued an edict that will affect the party in some way, such as new regulations or a call to arms.

## Dungeon Features (d20)
1. A hidden door leads to a secret passage filled with traps and puzzles.
2. A room is filled with ancient artifacts, each with its own unique power and history.
3. The party encounters a group of hostile undead who are guarding a valuable treasure.
4. A large, ornate chest is locked, requiring the party to find the key or solve a complex riddle.
5. The dungeon contains a magical portal that can transport them to other dimensions or planes of existence.
6. The walls of the dungeon are lined with ancient runes and symbols, providing clues about the layout and history of the place.
7. A room is filled with strange, glowing crystals that emit a powerful energy field when activated.
8. The party finds a hidden trapdoor leading to a secret chamber containing valuable treasure or information.
9. A large, ornate mirror reflects not only what's in front of it but also the past and future events within the dungeon.
10. The dungeon is filled with ancient puzzles that require the party to solve them before proceeding further.

## Treasure Hoard Contents (d20)
1. A chest containing a mix of gold, silver, and precious gems worth thousands of gold pieces.
2. A magical artifact that can grant its wielder increased strength or dexterity for a limited time.
3. A cursed item that brings bad luck to anyone who wields it, causing misfortune and accidents.
4. A collection of rare books on magic and alchemy that contain valuable knowledge and spells.
5. A set of enchanted weapons that can deal extra damage against certain types of enemies.
6. A potion that grants the drinker temporary invulnerability or enhanced abilities for a limited time.
7. A gemstone with a powerful enchantment, such as protection from evil or increased luck.
8. A collection of magical scrolls containing rare spells and incantations.
9. A chest filled with ancient coins from long-lost empires, each bearing unique engravings and symbols.
10. A set of mystical runes carved into a stone tablet that can be used to unlock hidden doors or reveal secret passages.

## Rumors (d20)
1. A mysterious figure is seen near the party's campfire at night, whispering about secrets and forbidden knowledge.
2. A local innkeeper overheard two travelers discussing a dangerous mission involving a powerful artifact.
3. A group of adventurers is rumored to be searching for a hidden temple filled with ancient treasures.
4. A wealthy merchant claims to have heard that a powerful sorcerer has been experimenting with dark magic in the nearby forest.
5. A local farmer reports strange occurrences on their land, suggesting the presence of supernatural entities or creatures.
6. The party overheard two guards discussing an upcoming attack by a rival group of adventurers.
7. A mysterious letter arrived at the party's campsite, containing cryptic clues about a hidden treasure.
8. A rumor spreads that a powerful wizard is offering a reward for anyone who can solve a complex puzzle or riddle.
9. The party encounters a group of travelers who claim to have seen a ghostly figure in the nearby ruins.
10. A local blacksmith has developed an unusual metal alloy, and some believe it holds magical properties.

## Weather Events (d20)
1. A sudden snowstorm traps the party in their current location, making travel difficult and dangerous.
2. The sky darkens as a powerful storm approaches, with lightning strikes and thunderous roars.
3. A sandstorm blows through the area, reducing visibility and causing disorientation.
4. A sudden rain shower turns paths into muddy quagmires, slowing the party's progress.
5. A heatwave raises temperatures to unbearable levels, making it difficult for the party to move quickly or remain alert.
6. The air thickens with a dense fog that makes navigation and communication challenging.
7. A hailstorm pelts the party with ice chunks, causing injuries and disrupting their plans.
8. An unexpected tornado touches down near the party's location, creating chaos and destruction in its wake.
9. A drought causes water sources to run dry, forcing the party to find alternative means of hydration or travel to a nearby oasis.
10. The ground shakes as an earthquake strikes, causing cracks and fissures that impede the party's progress.
