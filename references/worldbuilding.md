# Worldbuilding Toolkit

## Kingdom/Realm Generator Template (d20)
- Government: Monarchy, Oligarchy, Democracy, Theocracy, Anarchy, etc.
- Military: Standing army, Militia, Mercenaries, etc.
- Economy: Feudalism, Capitalism, Socialism, etc.
- Culture: Artistic, Religious, Technological, etc.
- Tensions: Internal conflicts, External threats, Rivalries, etc.

## City Generator Template (d20)
- Size: Small village, Medium town, Large city, Megacity
- Districts: Commercial, Residential, Industrial, Government, etc.
- Notable Locations: Market square, Cathedral, Guildhall, etc.
- Power Structure: Mayor, Lord, Council, etc.
- Problems: Overpopulation, Poverty, Corruption, etc.

## Village Generator Template (d20)
- Economy: Agriculture, Fishing, Mining, Trade, etc.
- Notable NPCs: Blacksmith, Innkeeper, Merchant, etc.
- Secret: Hidden treasure, Prophecy, Curse, etc.
- Recent Event: Festival, Disaster, Arrival of travelers, etc.

## Religion Template (d20)
- Deity: Nature, War, Knowledge, etc.
- Worship Style: Rituals, Pilgrimages, Festivals, etc.
- Temples: Grand cathedral, Hidden shrine, Outpost, etc.
- Holy Days: Celebrations, Fasts, Ceremonies, etc.
- Conflicts: Sects, Heresies, Religious wars, etc.

## Guild Templates (d20)
- Thieves' Guild: Art of stealth, Espionage, Lockpicking, etc.
  - Structure: Apprentices, Journeyman, Master Thief, etc.
  - Ranks: Novice, Apprentice, Journeyman, Master, etc.
  - Missions: Theft, Sabotage, Espionage, etc.
- Merchant Guild: Trade, Negotiation, Banking, etc.
  - Structure: Apprentices, Journeyman, Master Merchant, etc.
  - Ranks: Novice, Apprentice, Journeyman, Master, etc.
  - Missions: Trade expeditions, Market negotiations, etc.
- Mage Guild: Magic, Spells, Research, etc.
  - Structure: Apprentices, Journeyman, Master Mage, etc.
  - Ranks: Novice, Apprentice, Journeyman, Master, etc.
  - Missions: Spellcasting, Research projects, etc.
- Warrior Guild: Combat training, Tactics, Defense, etc.
  - Structure: Apprentices, Journeyman, Veteran, etc.
  - Ranks: Recruit, Soldier, Veteran, Commander, etc.
  - Missions: Training, Defending settlements, Expeditions, etc.

## Political Intrigue Generator (d20)
- Factions: Noble houses, Merchant guilds, Religious groups, etc.
- Goals: Power consolidation, Wealth accumulation, Control of resources, etc.
- Alliances: Forming coalitions, Seeking support from external powers, etc.
- Betrayals: Schemes, Double-crosses, False alliances, etc.

## Calendar System (d20)
- Months: Names and lengths
- Seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter
- Holidays: Religious, Cultural, Historical events
- Moon Phases: New moon, Full moon, Quarter moons

## History Timeline Template (d20)
- Ancient Era: Myths, Legends, Founding of kingdoms
- Middle Era: Wars, Revolutions, Great migrations
- Modern Era: Technological advancements, Social changes
- Recent Events: Recent conflicts, Political upheavals, Natural disasters

## Map Key Symbols (d20)
- Standard Cartography Legend: Mountains, Rivers, Forests, etc.
  - Symbols: ⛶ for mountains, ⚵ for rivers, ❄ for forests, etc.
- Additional Symbols: Ruins, Castles, Temples, etc.

## Languages and Scripts (d20)
- Common Language: Spoken by the majority of people in a region
  - Speakers: 80% of population, Used in daily life, Trade, etc.
- Exotic Language: Rarely spoken, Associated with magic or ancient cultures
  - Speakers: <1%, Used in rituals, writings, etc.
- Ancient Script: Long forgotten, Used in historical documents, inscriptions
  - Readers: <5%, Scholars, Historians, etc.
