Trap References - D&D 5e

SIMPLE TRAPS (10)
1. Trip wire: Trigger: Stepping on it. DC to detect: 12. DC to disarm: 14. Attack bonus: +7. Damage: 3 bludgeoning (or 2d6 piercing if using a blade). Reset: Releases after 1 minute or when triggered.
2. Pressure plate: Trigger: Stepping on it. DC to detect: 10. DC to disarm: 12. Attack bonus: +4. Damage: 2 bludgeoning. Reset: Releases after 1 minute or when triggered.
3. Pit trap: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 15. DC to disarm: 16. Attack bonus: +8. Damage: 2d6 piercing (or 4d6 if in water). Reset: Fills with water, takes 1 minute to refill.
4. Falling net: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 13. DC to disarm: 15. Attack bonus: +7. Damage: 2d6 piercing. Reset: Falls again after 1 minute or when triggered.
5. Poison darts: Trigger: Stepping on it. DC to detect: 14. DC to disarm: 16. Save DC: 14 (Poison). Damage: 3 poison damage per turn until cured. Reset: Darts vanish after use.
6. Scything blade: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 12. DC to disarm: 14. Attack bonus: +5. Damage: 1d8 slashing or 2d6 piercing if in water. Reset: Falls again after 1 minute or when triggered.
7. Collapsing ceiling: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 13. DC to disarm: 15. Attack bonus: +7. Damage: 4d6 bludgeoning. Reset: Takes 1 minute to collapse fully, then 1 minute to return.
8. Burning hands glyph: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 14. DC to disarm: 16. Save DC: 12 (Fire). Damage: 3 fire damage per turn until cured. Reset: Glyph extinguishes after 5 minutes or when triggered.
9. Alarm spell trap: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 10. DC to disarm: 12. Attack bonus: +4. Effect: Alarms sound, creatures flee (no damage). Reset: Resets after 1 minute with 1d4+3 seconds delay.
10. Bear trap: Trigger: Stepping on it. DC to detect: 15. DC to disarm: 17. Attack bonus: +9. Damage: 2d6 bludgeoning or 2d6 piercing if in water. Reset: Springs back after 3 minutes.

COMPLEX TRAPS (5)
1. Rolling boulder: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 18. DC to disarm: 20. Attack bonus: +10. Damage: 4d6 bludgeoning. Reset: Takes 1 minute to roll, then rolls again after 1 minute.
2. Flooding room: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 15. DC to disarm: 17. Effect: Room floods with water (DC 15 Dex save or 2d4 cold damage). Reset: Takes 3 minutes to dry, then floods again after 2 minutes.
3. Poison gas chamber: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 16. DC to disarm: 18. Effect: Poison gas (DC 15 Con save or 4d6 poison damage per round). Reset: Takes 5 minutes to clear, then releases gas again after 3 minutes.
4. Mechanical crusher: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 20. DC to disarm: 22. Effect: Crushes creatures (DC 18 Dex save or 6d6 bludgeoning). Reset: Takes 1 minute to reset, then triggers again after 30 seconds.
5. Reverse gravity trap: Trigger: Entering area. DC to detect: 20. DC to disarm: 24. Effect: Gravity reverses (DC 20 Dex save or 6d6 bludgeoning). Reset: Takes 1 minute to reverse, then triggers again after 30 seconds.
