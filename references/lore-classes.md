# Lore of Classes

## Barbarian
The Barbarian is a fierce warrior who channels primal rage and strength.

### Role
- **Role**: Frontline combatant, tank.
- **Key Ability**: Strength
- **Flavor**: Wild, untamed, and powerful. Often hails from tribal cultures or regions with harsh climates.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Path of the Ancestral Guardian**: Focuses on honoring ancestors and invoking their power in battle.
  - **Path of the Totem Warrior**: Emphasizes connection to natural spirits and elemental forces.
  - **Path of the Zealot**: Specializes in holy fury and divine wrath.

## Bard
The Bard is a versatile performer who uses music, charm, and magic to entertain and inspire.

### Role
- **Role**: Support, entertainer, diplomat.
- **Key Ability**: Charisma
- **Flavor**: Artistic, charismatic, and influential. Often hails from bardic colleges or traveling performers.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **College of Eloquence**: Focuses on persuasion and eloquent speech.
  - **College of Glamour**: Emphasizes illusionary magic and enchantment.
  - **College of Lore**: Specializes in magical knowledge and spellcasting.

## Cleric
The Cleric is a divine servant who channels the power of deities to heal, protect, and punish.

### Role
- **Role**: Healer, defender, enforcer.
- **Key Ability**: Wisdom
- **Flavor**: Devout, pious, and righteous. Often hails from temples or religious orders.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Domain of Life**: Focuses on healing and protection.
  - **Domain of Light**: Specializes in divine justice and punishment.
  - **Domain of Trickery**: Emphasizes manipulation and deception.

## Druid
The Druid is a nature mage who commands the forces of the natural world.

### Role
- **Role**: Nature guardian, healer, protector.
- **Key Ability**: Wisdom
- **Flavor**: Reverent, wise, and harmonious with nature. Often hails from druid circles or ancient forests.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Circle of the Land**: Focuses on land stewardship and elemental control.
  - **Circle of the Moon**: Specializes in lunar magic and astral alignment.
  - **Circle of Spores**: Emphasizes plant life, fungi, and decay.

## Fighter
The Fighter is a versatile warrior who excels in combat and strategy.

### Role
- **Role**: Frontline combatant, tactician.
- **Key Ability**: Strength or Dexterity
- **Flavor**: Disciplined, skilled, and adaptable. Often hails from military units or knightly orders.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Champion**: Focuses on martial prowess and honor.
  - **Eldritch Knight**: Emphasizes spellcasting and melee combat.
  - **Paladin of Glory**: Specializes in holy combat and divine protection.

## Monk
The Monk is a master of martial arts, using chi to enhance their physical abilities.

### Role
- **Role**: Frontline combatant, defender, healer.
- **Key Ability**: Wisdom or Dexterity
- **Flavor**: Disciplined, spiritual, and focused. Often hails from monasteries or martial arts schools.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Way of the Open Hand**: Focuses on non-lethal techniques and healing.
  - **Way of Shadow**: Emphasizes stealth and assassination.
  - **Way of the Four Elements**: Specializes in elemental chi control.

## Paladin
The Paladin is a holy warrior who serves as a divine champion, upholding justice and righteousness.

### Role
- **Role**: Frontline combatant, defender, enforcer.
- **Key Ability**: Wisdom or Strength
- **Flavor**: Devout, righteous, and noble. Often hails from orders of knights or religious institutions.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Oath of Devotion**: Focuses on protecting the innocent and upholding oaths.
  - **Oath of Vengeance**: Specializes in avenging wrongs and punishing evil.
  - **Oathbreaker Knight**: Emphasizes redemption and forgiveness.

## Ranger
The Ranger is a skilled hunter who roams wild lands, using nature and stealth to their advantage.

### Role
- **Role**: Frontline combatant, scout, tracker.
- **Key Ability**: Dexterity or Wisdom
- **Flavor**: Independent, resourceful, and natural. Often hails from wilderness regions or nomadic tribes.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Hunter**: Focuses on tracking and hunting.
  - **Beast Master**: Emphasizes companionship with animals.
  - **Druid of the Wild**: Specializes in druidic magic and nature connection.

## Rogue
The Rogue is a skilled criminal who excels in stealth, deception, and thievery.

### Role
- **Role**: Frontline combatant, support, infiltrator.
- **Key Ability**: Dexterity
- **Flavor**: Cunning, resourceful, and adaptable. Often hails from thieves' guilds or criminal organizations.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Arcane Trickster**: Emphasizes illusionary magic and stealth.
  - **Assassin**: Specializes in deadly strikes and espionage.
  - **Thief of Many Tricks**: Focuses on a wide range of thieving skills.

## Sorcerer
The Sorcerer is a spellcaster who channels innate magical power from an ancient lineage.

### Role
- **Role**: Support, damage dealer, utility.
- **Key Ability**: Charisma or Intelligence
- **Flavor**: Mysterious, unpredictable, and powerful. Often hails from families with a long history of sorcery.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Divine Soul**: Focuses on divine magic and holy spells.
  - **Wild Magic**: Emphasizes chaotic and unpredictable spellcasting.
  - **College of Omens**: Specializes in divination and augury.

## Warlock
The Warlock is a pact mage who trades their soul for magical power, using dark bargains to enhance their abilities.

### Role
- **Role**: Support, damage dealer, utility.
- **Key Ability**: Charisma or Intelligence
- **Flavor**: Mysterious, powerful, and sinister. Often hails from secret societies or ancient cults.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Eldritch Invoker**: Focuses on spellcasting and pact magic.
  - **Pact of the Chain**: Emphasizes control over familiars and summoned creatures.
  - **Pact of the Shadow**: Specializes in shadow magic and concealment.

## Wizard
The Wizard is a scholar who harnesses arcane knowledge to cast powerful spells.

### Role
- **Role**: Support, damage dealer, utility.
- **Key Ability**: Intelligence or Wisdom
- **Flavor**: Knowledgeable, analytical, and scholarly. Often hails from magical academies or ancient libraries.
- **Subclasses**:
  - **Arcane Archer**: Focuses on ranged spellcasting and archery.
  - **Archmage**: Specializes in high-level magic and arcane lore.
  - **Warlock of the Archdemons**: Emphasizes dark pact magic and summoning.
