# Monsters

## Goblin
**AC**: 13
**HP**: 8
**STR**: 10
**DEX**: 15
**CON**: 11
**INT**: 12
**WIS**: 10
**CHA**: 6

- **Speed**: 30 ft.
- **Damage Resistances**: Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks
- **Skills**: Stealth +5
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 10
- **Languages**: Common, Goblin
- **Challenge**: 1/4 (50 XP)

- **Actions**:
  - **Multiattack**: The goblin makes two melee attacks.
  - **Shortsword Attack**: *Melee Weapon Attack*: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit*: 7 (1d6+4) slashing damage.
  - **Rope Dart**: *Ranged Weapon Attack*: +6 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target. *Hit*: 9 (2d4+3) bludgeoning damage and the target must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or be restrained until the goblin ends its turn.
