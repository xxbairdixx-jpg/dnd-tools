# D&D 5e Character Creation Guide

## Step-by-step process
1. Choose a race (bonus ability scores and traits)
2. Select a class (hit dice, proficiencies, features)
3. Pick a background (proficiencies, tools, feature, characteristics)
4. Generate ability scores using standard array or point buy
5. Apply racial bonuses to your scores
6. Choose level (determines hit dice, experience points)
7. Finalize character with equipment and starting gear

## Ability score generation methods
- **Standard Array:** 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8 assigned to six abilities
- **Point Buy:** 20 points, each ability can gain up to +6
- **Rolling:** Each die d20 gives a score of 1d20+10

## Racial ability score bonuses (all SRD races)
- Elf: +2 Wisdom, +1 Dexterity, Darkvision
- Half-Elf: +2 Charisma and one other ability (+1 to two abilities), feathery hands
- Halfling: +2 Constitution, resistance to poison damage, darkvision
- Tiefling: +2 Charisma, darkvision, fiery breath (3d6 fire)
- Gnome: +2 Intelligence or Wisdom, darkvision, gnomish tools proficiency
- Aasimar: +10 Wisdom, radiant aura, celestial traits
- Dragonborn: +2 Strength, +1 to two abilities, draconic trait (fire, lightning, etc.)
- Tiefling: as above

## Class hit dice, proficiencies, and starting equipment (all 11 classes)

**Barbarian:** d8 hit die, proficiency in Athletics, unarmored armor, rage feature
**Bard:** d8 hit die, Proficiency in two skills (musical instrument, dance), spellcasting (2nd-level spells)
**Cleric:** d6 hit die, Proficiency in Religion and one other skill, domain features
**Druid:** d8 hit die, Proficiency in Druidry, nature magic, beast familiar
**Fighter:** d10 hit die, proficiency in two skills (Athletics, Weapon Mastery), unarmored armor
**Monk:** d8 hit die, Acrobatics proficiency, ki, monk spells
**Paladin:** d8 hit die, Proficiency in Religion and one other skill, Oath of Devotion or Oath of Vengeance
**Rogue:** d6 hit die, two skills (Sleight of Hand, Stealth), Thieves' tools
**Sorcerer:** d4 hit die, no proficiencies, innate magic
**Warlock:** d8 hit die, Eldritch Blast, Pact Magic
**Wizard:** d8 hit die, Proficiency in Arcana and one other skill, spellcasting

## Experience point table (levels 1-20)
Level: XP gained per level
1: 50, 2: 70, 3: 90, 4: 110, 5: 130, 6: 150, 7: 180, 8: 210, 9: 240, 10: 280
11: 330, 12: 370, 13: 410, 14: 450, 15: 500, 16: 550, 17: 600, 18: 650, 19: 720, 20: 800

## Proficiency bonus by level
Level 1-3: +0, Level 4-5: +1, Level 6-7: +2, Level 8-9: +3, Level 10+: +4

## Multiclassing requirements and rules
- Can have maximum 20 levels total
- Must be proficient in skills from both classes (except Sorcerer/Warlock)
- Cannot multiclass at level 1 for most classes
- Some classes cannot take levels beyond certain limits
- Requires proficiency in two skill trees
