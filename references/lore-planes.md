# Lore of the Planes

## Material Plane
The Material Plane is the realm we inhabit and understand. It is a world of matter, life, and magic.

### Notable Inhabitants
- Humans
- Elves (High/Wood/Drow)
- Dwarves (Hill/Mountain)
- Halflings
- Gnomes
- Half-Elves
- Half-Orcs
- Tieflings
- Dragonborn
- Various monsters and creatures.

### Connections
- **Feywild**: The Material Plane borders the Feywild, a realm of magic and wonder. Travelers can enter the Feywild through portals or by crossing the borderlands.
- **Shadowfell**: The Shadowfell is a twisted reflection of the Material Plane, where the dead walk and shadows take on life. It serves as a bridge to the afterlife.

## Feywild
The Feywild is a realm of magic, wonder, and enchantment. It exists in harmony with the Material Plane but remains hidden from most mortals.

### Notable Inhabitants
- Faeries
- Dryads
- Satyrs
- Will-O'-the-Wisps
- Various fey creatures.

## Shadowfell
The Shadowfell is a twisted, dark realm where the dead and undead walk. It serves as a bridge to the afterlife but is not a pleasant place for the living.

### Notable Inhabitants
- Liches
- Demons
- Devils
- Undead creatures (ghasts, wights, wraiths)
- Various malevolent spirits and entities.

## Elemental Planes
The Elemental Planes are realms of pure elemental energy. Each plane represents a different element: Fire, Water, Air, Earth, and Energy.

### Notable Inhabitants
- Fire Elementals (Flamewrought, Ashen, Ember)
- Water Elementals (Aquanauts, Sea Striders, Deep Dwellers)
- Air Elementals (Windwraiths, Stormriders, Cloudwalkers)
- Earth Elementals (Stonefists, Rockknights, Stoneborn)
- Energy Elementals (Forcebringers, Radiant Beings, Voidwalkers)

### Connections
- **Astral Sea**: The Astral Sea connects the Elemental Planes and serves as a conduit for magic. Travelers can move between planes via this sea.
- **Outer Planes**: The Outer Planes are realms of law and chaos, where souls go after death. They include regions like the Nine Hells, Mount Celestia, and Mechanus.

## Astral Sea
The Astral Sea is a vast expanse of starlight that connects all planes of existence. It serves as a conduit for magic and a pathway between worlds.

### Notable Inhabitants
- Angels
- Demons
- Devils
- Archons
- Other cosmic entities.

## Ethereal Plane
The Ethereal Plane is a realm of pure energy, existing in a state between solid matter and the Astral Sea. It serves as a liminal space for astral travel and magic.

### Notable Inhabitants
- Ethereal beings (Ethereals, Ethereals of Law, Ethereals of Chaos)
- Ghosts
- Specters
- Wraiths
- Various spirits and incorporeal entities.
